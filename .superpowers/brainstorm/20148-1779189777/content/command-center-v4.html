Waveform State Transitions

Idle: All bars collapse to 2px flat lines, desaturated to dim gray. Silent, minimal. The orb goes slate-gray, ring stops orbiting.

Listening (SPACE held / always-on triggered): Bars spring to life — animated heights driven by real mic amplitude. Cyan gradient. The orb lights up cyan, ring starts orbiting with tracking particle. SPACE key glows. Hint changes to "stop".

Processing: Bars freeze at last amplitude, fade to gold/amber. Orb shifts to gold.

Speaking (TTS): Bars animate to TTS audio waveform. Orb shifts to violet.

Everything Changes Together

The orb color, ring animation, waveform bars, status dot, status text, SPACE key glow, border glow, and system stat brightness all transition together based on state. Idle = dim everything. Active = light up everything. One coherent visual pulse across the entire UI.
